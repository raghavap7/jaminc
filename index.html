Our Products

Paymently is our proprietary product, it helps online and mobile businesses around the world accept credit card payments by providing a merchant account, payment gateway, recurring billing and credit card storage.

Paymently is a SAAS application. The term SAAS stands for Software As A Service. Typical SAAS providers deliver common business applications online, which are accessed, from a web browser, while the software and data are stored on servers

As part of your Paymently account, you may create an unlimited number of subscription plans. You may choose to offer a variety of different billing cycles, and/or pricing tiers to provide adequate choice for your customers.
